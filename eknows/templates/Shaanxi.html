Ten Deadliest Earthquake

• 1556: Shaanxi, China

Date: January 23, 1556
Time: Estimated to be early in the morning.
Epicenter: Wei River Valley in Shaanxi Province, near Huazhou, Weinan and Huayin.
Magnitude: 8.0
Summary of Damages: It estimates around 830,000 people were killed and injured in this earthquake. It reduced the population of two provinces by the 60 percent because of the massive death toll
