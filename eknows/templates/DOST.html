DOST mandates to provide central direction, leadership and coordination of scientific and technological efforts. DOST is known to be the provider of world-class scientific, technological and innovative solutions that will lead to higher productivity. It was originally established as NSDB (National Science and Development Board) at 1958, but in 1981, it was reconstituted as NSTA (National Science and Technology Authority)
PHILVOLCS is under the institute of DOST in which they are capable to share knowledge about the earthquakes, volcanic eruptions and tsunami. Last December 6, DOST-PHIVOLCS talks about the volcano hazards on science week. They continously monitoring the active volcanos that might suspected in eruption that could cause frequent earthquakes in certain places. They also highlighted the web-based and application tools for preparedness in a disaster during their webinar series entitled of "Practical tools on Hazards Assessment for Individual and Family Preparedness"last Novemeber 26, as a part of 2021 National Science and Technology Week.
