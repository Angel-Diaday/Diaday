Top Ten Strongest Earthquake to hit the Philippines

• 1976: Magnitude 8.0 in Mindanao

Date and Time: August 17, 1976, 12:11 AM PST
Epicenter: 60 miles offshore from Cotabato City
Description: A devastating earthquake caused on the island of Mindanao with destructive tsunami generated in Moro Gulf and in Celebes Sea. It is one of the worst earthquake and tsunami occur in the Philippine History. Earthquake occur past midnight, causing the loss of lives is reduced.
Damages: Most of the buildings are severely damaged due to the poor construction or inadequate foundations. 8,000 people loss their lives where 90% of deaths are because of tsunami.
