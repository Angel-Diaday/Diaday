A History of Earthquake in the Philippines

• 2013 Bohol Earthquake

Date: October 15, 2013
Time: 00:12 UTC (08:12 PST)
Epicenter: 6 kilometers southwest of Sagbayan.
Magnitude: 7.2
Summary of Damages: 1.2 million people are affected, 222 people died and 976 are injured. 79,000 structures are damaged including the public buildings and roads. The total cost of damages were 2.25 billion Philippine pesos.
