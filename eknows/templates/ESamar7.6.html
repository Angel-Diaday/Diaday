Top Ten Strongest Earthquake to hit the Philippines

• 2012: Magnitude 7.6 near Eastern Samar

Date and Time: August 31,2012, 12:47 AM PST
Epicenter: 93 kilometers east of Sulangan, Eastern Samar
Description: Since the earthquake is far from the coast, it cause less damage than the other earthquakes. Also, there is no major tsunami occured within the area, but only a high tide.
Damages: As there is no tsunami, it has only one person died and two injured people inside their house. 110 total houses are damaged, 78 are totally wrecked and 32 were partially damaged.
