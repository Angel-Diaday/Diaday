Top Ten Strongest Earthquake to hit the Philippines

• 2002: Magnitude 7.5 in Southern Mindanao

Date and Time: March 6, 2002, 5:15 AM PST
Epicenter: 81 kilometers of Isulan, Sultan Kudarat
Description: The ground shaking intensifies at different provinces. Highest intensity were in Palimbang, Sultan Kundarat which is under Intensity IX. Meanwhile, Maltum Saranggani and Lebak, Sultan Kudarat are under Intensity VIII.
Damages: 7,684 families were affected under the four provinces, and 17 municipalities. Eight people died and 41 are injured. Seven bridges, two health centers are destroyed with the total of 4.175 million pesos.
