Emergency Kit

Emergency kits are one must to have in a house. It involves the food, water and other important necessities. It needs a big bag or a duffel bag to put these things. Here is the basic list of what you should pack in a emergency kit:
• Water - one gallon or more for drinking and sanitation.
• Food - the most essential. Non-perishable food is better like canned goods, noodles, and rice.
• Battery-powered radio and flashlight.
• Whistle - a signal for help.
• Extra batteries.
• Wrench or pliers.
• Important documents - such as banknotes, passports and other files.
