• 1968 Casiguran Earthquake

Date: August 2, 1968
Time: 20:19 UTC (04:19 PST)
Epicenter: Casiguran town in Aurora
Magnitude: 7.3
Summary of Damages: 268 people are killed. Infrastructures and establishments are destroyed including the Aloha Theater, National Library and the Liwayway Hotel in Manila.
