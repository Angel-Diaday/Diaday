A History of Earthquake in the Philippines

• 1976 Moro Gulf Earthquake

Date: August 17, 1976
Time: 16:11 UTC (00:11 PST)
Epicenter: 60 miles offshore from Cotabato City
Magnitude: 7.9
Summary of Damages: A tsunami occured after the earthquake that almost 8,000 people died and injured. Some of them went missing. Buildings and infrastructures have severe damages including the schools, hotels, churches and theaters.
