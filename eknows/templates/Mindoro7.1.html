Top Ten Strongest Earthquake to hit the Philippines

• 1994: Magnitude 7.1 in Mindoro

Date and Time: November 15, 1994, 03:15 AM PST
Epicenter: Northwest of Baco, Mindoro
Description: In Oriental Mindoro, earthquake and tsunami occur together with the 5 minutes interval. Tsunami struck to the northern and eastern shoreline of Mindoro island.
Damages: A tsunami killed 78 people, 430 are injured and 7,566 houses in 13 municipalities are damaged. 24 bridges and numerous fishing boats are also destroyed. Most of the people died are the children under 10 years old and senior citizens.
