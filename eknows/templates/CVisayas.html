Top Ten Strongest Earthquake to hit the Philippines

• 2012: Magnitude 6.9 in Central Visayas

Date and Time: February 6, 2012, 11:49 AM PST
Epicenter: Tayasan, Negros Oriental
Description: It has depth of ten kilometers. Has the latitude of 9.97° north and 123.14° east. Butuan and Cagayan de Oro are under Intensity III despite how far it is to the epicenter.
Damages: PHIVOLCS (Philippine Volcanology and Seismology) alerted the people of Negros for the Tsunami Level Alert II. 52 people died and 29 were buried 30 feet under. Seven bridges were damaged and some infrastructures has light damages.
