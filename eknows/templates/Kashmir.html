Ten Deadliest Earthquake

• 2005: Kashmir, Pakistan

Date: October 8, 2005
Time: 03:50 UTC (08:50 in local time)
Epicenter: 12 miles northwest of Muzaffarabad.
Magnitude: 7.6
Summary of Damages: The earthquake killed more than 80,000 people, with 100,000 injured and three million are homeless. There are 32,000 buildings collapsed in Kashmir.
