What to do after an earthquake?

• Check yourself first if you are have scratches or injuries then apply the first aid needed.
• Check your surroundings for other hazards and control them, such as possible collapse, fire and chemical spills.
• Check to the cracks and damages of the foundation of the building or in your home.
• Turn on radio or battery operated device to listen in emergency announcements. You can also check through the internet.
• Keep the streets free from the the disturbances, in case for emergency vehicles.
• Injured victims shouldn't move from one place to another to prevent from the future injuries. Call a help immediately, especially if it is a serious injury.
• Make plan on how to evacuate people in a safe place incase there is a strong aftershocks or a tsunami alert.
• Prepare for possible aftershocks. If occurs, find a safe spot temporarily.
• Stay calm and help people.
