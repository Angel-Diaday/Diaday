First Aid Kit

Earthquakes may occur in different circumstances. With the first aid kit, you may lessen the injuries of the injured people before a help came that way. Here is the list of what inside the first aid kit:
• Adhesive bandages in all sizes, gauge pad or gauge roll
• Scissors and tweezer
• Examination gloves
• Alcohol and alcohol pads
• Cotton Swabs
• Betadine
• Inhaler (for asthma)
