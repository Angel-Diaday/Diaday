What to do during an earthquake?

• Know the basic steps; which is the duck, cover, and hold.
• Hide in the table and stop posting on social media sites.
• Cover your head up onto the nape a book or any other sturdy things to prevent head injury.
• If indoors, find and take cover a desk, table and other furniture to prevent you from falling objects.
• Don't move while it is still shaking. Stay where you are, and think about your plan of action after the shaking, then move.
• Use stairs and emergency exits. It is not safe to use a elevator because power lines may broke and you may stuck inside the elevator.
• If outdoors, go to an empty field. Avoid tall buildings, power lines and tall trees.
• If driving, check your surroundings if it is safe to stop your vehicle. Stay inside your vehicle until the shaking stops.
• If you are driving in a overpass, underpass and a bridge. Get out of the car immediately.
• If power lines have fallen across, do not touch any kind of metals. Get away from the power lines and call help immediately.
