Ten Deadliest Earthquake

• 1970: Chimbote, Peru

Date: May 31, 1970
Time: 03:23 UTC (03:23 in local time)
Epicenter: 15 miles west of Chimbote
Magnitude: 7.9
Summary of Damages: 70,000 people are killed and 50,000 are injured leaving 800,000 people are being homeless after the disaster. In term of infrastructures, 200,000 homes and buildings are roughly destroyed.
