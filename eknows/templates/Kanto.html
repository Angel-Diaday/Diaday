Ten Deadliest Earthquake

• 1923: Kanto, Japan

Date: September 1, 1923
Time: 02:58 UTC (11:58 in local time)
Epicenter: Near Oshima Island in Sagama Bay
Magnitude: 7.9
Summary of Damages: Many infrastructure has been damaged especially in Yokohoma, Chiba, Kanagawa, Shizuoka, and Kanto region. About 800 people died and 570,000 homes were destroyed with total cost of estimated to 1 billion US dollars.
