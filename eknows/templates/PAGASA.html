Philippine Atmospheric Geophysical Astronomical Services and Administration
Date Founded: December 8, 1972
Executive Director: Sec. Vicenter B. Malano, PhD.
Headquarters: PAGASA Science Garden Complex, BIR Road, Brgy. Central, Quezon City, Manila
Hotlines: (02)8284-0800
Website: https://www.pagasa.dost.gov.ph/
PAGASA provide science and technology-based assessments to decision-making with the relevant concerns such as the disaster risk reduction, climate change adaptation and integrated water resources management. They deliver reliable and relevant weather-related information resilient to typhoons, floods, landlides, storm surges and other natural disasters.
PAGASA's duties is to maintain observation and forecasting network that gives data to analyze and prepare for possible circumstances. They give forecasts, bulletins, and warnings to the public. PAGASA, NDRRMC and other government agency and department, collaborate to build the "Enhancing Risk Analysis Capabilities for Flood, Tropical Cyclone Severe Wind and Earthquake for Greater Metro Manila" that tackles on what will the emergency plans to do when natural hazard may occur in Metro Manila.
