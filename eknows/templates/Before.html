What to do before an earthquake?

• Develop a earthquake plan together with your family. Including where you will reunite if you are seperated to each other when the earthquake occurs.
• Choose a relative or a close friend to call about your whereabouts and condition.
• If you are inside the building, know the safe spots like sturdy tables, desks and against inside walls.
• Avoid the danger spots like windows, mirrors, hanging objects and unsecured furniture that may cause your life is in danger.
• Keep your flammable or hazardous chemicals in the lower shelves.
• Prepare an emergency bag that includes emergency food, water and other essential necessities. Check them every 6 months for the expiration date.
• Conduct practice drills on how to keep yourself safe from the possible dangers.
• Learn first aid and the cardiopulmonary resuscitation (CPR) from Red Cross or any other non-government organization.
• Secure your surroundings. Check your cabinets, and shelves if they are sturdy enough. Replace the other furniture that may fall from shaking the ground.
