Ten Most Powerful Earthquake in Earth History

• Maule Earthquake (2010) - 8.8 Magnitude

Date: February 27, 2010
Time: 06:34 UTC (03:34 in local time)
Epicenter: 200 miles southwest of Santiago, Chile
Summary of Damages: Several coastal cities in south-central Chile have been devastated by the tsunami caused by the earthquake. Buildings collapsed and many people died in many cities. Authorities announced in January 2011 the final death toll of 525 casualties and 25 missing.
