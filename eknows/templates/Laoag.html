A History of Earthquake in the Philippines

• 1983 Laoag Earthquake

Date: August 17, 1983
Time: 12:17 UTC (20:17 PST)
Epicenter: 30 aerial kilometers east-northeast of Laoag City.
Magnitude: 6.5
Summary of Damages: Several buildings in seven towns were totally collapsed, including the Laoag Cathedral and Bojeador Lighthouse. 16 people died and 47 are injured. Electricity was also cut off in Laoag after the disaster.
