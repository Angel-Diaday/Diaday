Ten Deadliest Earthquake

• 1976: Tangshan, China

Date: July 28, 1976
Time: 03:42 AM in local time
Epicenter: 68 miles east of Beijing
Magnitude: 7.5
Summary of Damages: Considered as one of the highest death toll with 655,000 are killed, 700,000 are injured and property damage were extensive. Most of the deaths were due to the collapse masonry house.
