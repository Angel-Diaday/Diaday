Top Ten Strongest Earthquake to hit the Philippines

• 1968: Magnitude 7.3 in Casiguran

Date and Time: August 2, 1968, 4:19 PM PST
Epicenter: Casiguran, Quezon (now part of Aurora Province)
Description: Considered as the most severe and most destructive earthquake experience during the last of 20 years. Landslides and fissures were observed in the mountainous part. Tsunami also recorded in the Japan's tide gauge station.
Damages: It costs of million dollars to the property damage. 270 people were killed and 261 are injured. Building have severe damages like the Philippine Bar Association, and the Old Philippine National Bank.
