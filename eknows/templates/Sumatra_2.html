Ten Most Powerful Earthquake in Earth History

• Sumatra Earthquake (2011) - 9.1 Magnitude

Date: December 26, 2011
Time: 05:46 UTC (14:46 in local time)
Epicenter: West coast of Northern, Sumatra
Summary of Damages: As a result of the earthquake, a tsunami occur. First waves are around 30 meters in 20 minutes, killing more than 100,000 people and ruining the city. Continuing, the tsunami struck the coasts of Thailand, India and Sri Lanka, killing tens of thousands more. A total of nearly 230,000 people have died.
