Headquarters: NDRRMC Building, Camp Aguinaldo, Quezon City
Hotlines: 8911 5061 to 65 (Trunk Line) Local: 100
Website: https://ndrrmc.gov.ph/
The National Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Council (NDRRMC), or formerly known as the National Disaster Coordinating Council (NDCC) consisting of different groups of various government, non-government, civil sector and the private sector organizations of the Philippine government. It established by the Republic Act 10121 of 2009 or known as the "Philippine Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Act of 2010."
NDRRMC plans and leads the activities in terms of field of communication, warning signals, emergency, transportation, evacuation rescue and auxilliary services like fire fighting and the police in the country. Last September 9, 2021, NDRRMC conducted a school-based disaster risk reduction and management webinar, in line with the National Simultaneous Earthquake Drill (NSED). Department of Environment and Natural Resources, and the Armed Forces of the Philippines is under NDRRMC.
