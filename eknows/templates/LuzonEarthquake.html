• 1990 Luzon Earthquake

Date: July 16, 1990
Time: 08:26 UTC(16:26 PST)
Epicenter: Near the town of Rizal, Nueve Ecija, northeast of Cabanatuan
Magnitude: 7.8
Summary of Damages: One of the most destructive earthquake occurs resulting to several collapsed buidings with 369 million US dollars of damages and total of 2,412 people are killed.
