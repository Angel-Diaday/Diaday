Top Ten Strongest Earthquake to hit the Philippines

• 2013: Magnitude 7.2 in Bohol

Date and Time: October 15, 2013, 8:12 AM PST
Epicenter: Island Province of Bohol
Description: Three weeks after the Typhoon Yolanda, (International name: Typhoon Haiyan,) a 7.2 magnitude earthquake occur. It was the deadliest earthquake in 23 years. It can compare it to 32 Hiroshima bombs exploded.
Damages: Since the province is not adjusted well from the typhoon, the earthquake affected 1.2 million people, including 222 people died, 976 are injured and eight went missing. 10,020 were severely damaged infrastructure and 35,621 are totally damaged.
