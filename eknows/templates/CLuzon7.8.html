Top Ten Strongest Earthquake to hit the Philippines

• 1990: Magnitude 7.8 in Central Luzon

Date and Time: July 16, 1990, 4:26 PM PST
Epicenter: Near Rizal Town, North East of Cabanatuan
Description: A 7.8 jolted to the Luzon, affecting the northern and central Luzon including southern Luzon and Manila. 23 provinces are severely affected but the most affected is the City of Baguio.
Damages: 2,412 deaths, 321 are missing and 3,513 are injured. In terms of structure, 25,305 are completely destroyed and 77, 429 are partly damaged. One of the infrastructures that has been severely damaged is the famous Hyatt Hotel. 695,000 US dollars are the total damages and losses.
