• Kamchatka, Russia Earthquake (1952) - 9.0 Magnitude

Date: November 4, 1952
Time: 16:58 GMT (04:58 in local time)
Epicenter: 52.8° N, 159.5° E
Summary of Damages: Estimated at $17 million USD, and includes a great deal of damage to shorelines and infrastructure. The highest waves were observed on the north shore of Oahu Island, they were nearly 15 feet in height.
